"We make a living by what we get,

But we make a life by what we give"



We the smile keepers have started with a small initiative to spread love and smiles across Goa.
All we wish is to make this world a happy place.
We wish to visit orphanages, old age homes and many other places;
We wish to distribute food items, clothes, and all the essential services to all those in need;
We aim at making everyone smile with few little things that we can give ❤

We wish nothing but a little support from all those who wish to do so!
